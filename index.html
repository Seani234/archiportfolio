About Me

What made me pursue architecture was my love for buildings, particularly renovation shows and buildings. At that time, I was so amazed with the beauty of architecture. I wondered how fast architects think when they need to build clients houses or even bigger buildings. It always fascinated me, and I was so curious on what they learn as part of their professional life. Nowadays, I see that an architect has the power to build structures that impact human life, with regards to various concepts synthesizing and boiling down to what we see everywhere, from schools, hospitals, to even our homes.
